About Picfie

Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still.

Hunt the view-the leiothrixx shoot is the photography competition to shoot our annual festival in a best possible manner as we do every year (leiothrixx-o-graphy) and managed by our picfie team.

Capturing the moments of leiothrixx is probably the best way to memorise these special moments of ANDC students and this is what we'll try to do in our event.
